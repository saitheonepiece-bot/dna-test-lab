How it works

What is QuizGen AI?

QuizGen AI turns any topic into a ready-to-take quiz in seconds, using AI right inside your web browser. There's no account to make for the site, nothing to download, and no cost.

✍️

1 · Type a topic

Enter anything you'd like to be quizzed on — a school subject, a hobby, a book, a country. Then choose how many questions (5–10) and how hard.

🤖

2 · The AI writes it

The built-in AI instantly creates multiple-choice questions — four answers each — and quietly marks which one is correct.

🏆

3 · Take & score

Answer the questions, hit submit, and get an instant score with every answer shown right or wrong. You can even edit the questions yourself.

Behind the scenes, QuizGen AI sends your topic to a free AI service, gets back the questions, and saves your quiz privately on your own device — so it's there when you come back.

🔒 About the free AI sign-in

The AI is powered by a free service called Puter. The first time you generate a quiz, a small Puter window may pop up asking you to sign in with a free Puter account. Here's exactly what that is — and why it's safe:

  • It's what makes the AI free. Instead of the website paying for every quiz, Puter ties the usage to your own free account. Everyday use costs you nothing.
  • No payment, ever. You're never asked for a credit card or any money — a free account is all it takes.
  • QuizGen AI never sees your login. You sign in to Puter directly, in their own secure popup. This website only receives the finished quiz — never your email or password.
  • Only your topic is shared. The one thing sent to the AI is the topic you type. Your finished quizzes stay saved on your device, not on any server.
  • You stay in control. Don't want to sign in? Just close the popup — you simply won't get an AI quiz that time. Nothing gets installed on your computer.

Simple guidelines: only ever sign in through the official Puter popup (never type a Puter password into any other box), you can use any email you like, and normal everyday use is free. Puter is a real, established company that provides free cloud and AI tools for websites like this one — it is not a virus, a scam, or anything harmful.
